Privacy Policy

Kirpa Home Care ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, families, and website visitors. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you provide directly, such as your name, phone number, email address, and details about the care services you're interested in, when you submit a contact form or call our office.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, develop care plans, and communicate about our services. We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

Health Information

Any health-related information shared with our care team is treated as confidential and is used solely for the purpose of delivering appropriate, safe care, in accordance with applicable Alberta health privacy legislation.

Cookies & Website Data

Our website may use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymized where possible and used only to improve the website experience.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at Kirpahomecare@gmail.com.
