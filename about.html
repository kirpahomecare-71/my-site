Our Story

Built on a Simple Belief: Everyone Deserves Dignified Care at Home

Kirpa Home Care was founded to give Edmonton families an alternative to impersonal, high-turnover care agencies. The word "kirpa" means grace and compassion — values we put into practice with every visit, every care plan, and every conversation with the families we serve.

We saw too many seniors and their families struggling to find caregivers who understood their language, their culture, and their specific health needs. So we built a team that does exactly that — combining licensed clinical expertise with genuine cultural fluency, all delivered in the comfort of home.

Today, Kirpa Home Care supports families across Edmonton and the surrounding region with personal care, specialized nursing, palliative support, companionship, and respite services — backed by a team that treats every client like family.

Family First Every care plan is designed around the individual, never a checklist.
What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, high-quality home care that allows seniors to age safely, comfortably, and with dignity — while helping families access the government funding available to ease the financial load.

Our Vision

To be Edmonton's most trusted name in home care — a place where every individual is empowered to age with dignity, surrounded by people who feel like family.

Our Values

Compassion first, always. We lead with empathy and treat every client and family member with warmth, patience, honesty, and unwavering respect.

Our Team

A Multilingual, Multicultural Care Team

Communication is the foundation of comfort. Our caregivers and care coordinators speak English, Punjabi, Gujrati and Hindi — meaning your loved one is cared for by someone who can truly understand them, not just translate for them.

  • Background-checked and reference-verified caregivers
  • Ongoing training in dementia care, fall prevention & more
  • Cultural and dietary sensitivity built into every care plan
